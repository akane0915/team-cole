Cole Hexum was diagnosed on September 13th, 2012 with Stage 4, Burkitt’s Leukemia at the age of 7.


After a long battle, Cole defeated his attacker and was released from the doctor’s care on February 4th, 2013.


During his battle, Cole made the decision to help other kids that have to face this obstacle in life.  The overwhelming amount of support that Cole received opened his eyes to what can be done, if only someone steps up and takes the lead.  Cole has decided to do just that.  Cole borrowed his beloved Oregon Duck’s motto of “Win the Day” and has been doing it ever since.


This site is dedicated to just that purpose.
