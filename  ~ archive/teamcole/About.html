Cole’s goal is to help families with support in many ways.  Some of the ways he would like to help is by orchestrating visits from local celebrities.  He has been working with all of the local sports teams in order to help make some wishes come true.  When he went through his bout, he had some very unique supporters from the local sports teams and knows how much it can brighten your day. 

Fundraising ideas and help is something that can help every support group.  It doesn’t have to be for the financial benefit of the family alone.  It is very therapeutic for the family’s friends.  Everyone in the support structure wants to help, sometimes this is the best way.
